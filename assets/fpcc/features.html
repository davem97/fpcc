Multiple Weapons

Get right into the asset with 5+ weapons that can be immediately tested, including a pistol, rifle, shotgun and sniper.

Simple Management

It comes with a simple weapon management system that stores all of the weapons used for easy access and modification.

Visual Feedback

Different features in the main scene can be used to test the weapons and receive visual feedback for testing purposes.

Recoil and Aiming System

The asset features a custom recoil system implemented to handle customizable vertical and horizontal recoil for both hipfire and aiming down sights.

Idle Sway & Recovery

Includes realistic idle sway, recoil recovery, and random recoil patterns for each gun, making gunplay feel dynamic.

Highly Customizable

Features different recoil based on whether you are hipfiring or aiming down sights, all easily tweaked in the editor.

Ballistics

There are bullet ballistics present in this asset which include bullet velocity and drop affected by physics and gravity.

No Rigidbody

There is no rigidbody present and the bullets can be fired at any desired speed with every feature being customizable.

Projectile Trajectory

The system also comes with simple projectile trajectory calculations perfect for arrows, grenades, and other throwables.
